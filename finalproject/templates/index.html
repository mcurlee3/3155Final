Our Home Our Purpose

COVID-19 changed the world and the way we live in it. What did not change, are the factors that bring us all Happiness, no matter where we live in the world. At Happy Home we have gathered data from the World Happiness Report, an annual culmination of sentiment results taken by Gallup World Poll surveys. Our data allows the user to travel the world looking for the values that bring her the most happiness, in an informative, intuitive, and easy to use format.

A Word From The Developers

Our Promise And Vision

As developers we want our product to become a hub for people looking to live outside of their home country. We accomplish this by providing easy to understand graphics with a fountain of country specific information. Users can explore values such as GDP, Freedom and even corruption. We have tools relevant not only to individuals, but companies, and realtors as well.
