Our Home Our Purpose

Every citizen of the world has been impacted by the effects of COVID-19 in some fashion. Many have been forced to work from home. We present a set of concrete statistical data based on averages of sentiment captured during the pandemic. Our users will be able to explore prospective living destinations based on values that they choose. Users will also be offered the choice of different visual formats based on selected fields of interest.

Our Promise And Vision

As developers we want our product to become a hub for people looking to live outside of their home country. To become a hub our product will combine easy to understand graphics with a plethora of country specific information. To become a true hub we will link to other sources that cover the countries within our database like blogs, realtors, and government websites.
